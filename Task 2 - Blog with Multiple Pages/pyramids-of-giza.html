Great Pyramids of Giza

The Great Pyramids of Giza are the most iconic of Egypt's ancient monuments.

They were built during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom of Ancient Egypt, around 2580 - 2560 BCE, and were originally covered in casing stones made of highly polished Tura limestone.
